Why Choose Personal Chef

Whether you are a busy parent with little time left in your day to cook or someone who loves to entertain but just can't figure out how to entertain and cook at the same time, a personal chef may be exactly what you are looking for. A personal chef will customize a menu to your specific tastes, go shopping for ingredients, prepare and cook those ingredients, interact with you and your guests explaining the dishes and cooking techniques used all while leaving your kitchen as clean as it was when they arrived.

Services

Busy Week

Prepare an entire weeks worth of made from scratch meals so your family can reheat at your own convenience during your busy week.

Dinner Parties

Prepare an elegant 4 course plated dinner or enjoy passed small bites for fun a night with friends or family.

Lessons

Provide cooking lessons to those who love cooking and want to learn insider chef secrets.

About Me

Achef is really only as good as the ingredients he cooks with. That is why Chef Derek Melander focuses on seasonal farm to table cooking with only the freshest of ingredients, sourcing local and in season ingredients whenever possible. With over 15 years culinary experience in New York City as a chef, he has extensive knowledge in many types of cuisines and techniques. With Chef Derek, you will get a highly personalized meal, or set of meals, tailored to your specific needs and dietary requests. He also loves interacting with guests during events, making sure to answer any questions they may have about the menu or cooking in general.

Chef Derek holds a bachelors degree from the University of Delaware and a Swiss higher diploma from SSTH in Switzerland, both for hospitality, as well as a culinary degree from the Institute of Culinary Education in New York City. He holds a food safety certificate from ServeSafe, a nationally recognized leader in food safety education, and has also had extensive on the job training in food safety. You can rest assure that your meal was not only delicious but also prepared and stored in the safest way possible.
